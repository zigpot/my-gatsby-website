Have You Seen the Man Under the Bridge?

Fariz A.
2023-10-19

have you seen the man under the bridge?
he leaves four in the morning
and before dawn he returns

have you seen the man under the bridge?
a lousy wooden cart is his company
and plastic bags filled with treasures
a man's trash is another man's treasure
for every human sized bag worth him a solid penny

have you seen the man under the bridge?
he's short and dark and skinny
but the arms are strong
for not only he carry weights,
but as well the fate of his family

have you seen the man under the bridge?
his legs are small
but the steps are certain
the hot barren asphalt roads greet his soles
Merciful men and women had offered him shoes and flip flops,
to his kind and gentle refusal.
For rubber soles goes thin over time, he says
and calluses goes thick over time

have you seen the man under the bridge?
he loves to be greeted
he desires to be respected
but he refused to be pitied
for he's not unemployed or physically limited
his food isn't as tasty as some others
and his clothes aren't as shiny
and his cart isn't as fast as those passing above the bridge
he just make less
not nothing

have you seen the man under the bridge?
he returns and immediately seek out for his children
used to be one,
two,
three,
almost four,
miscarriage,
three,
dengue fever,
two
eldest got married,
so now just one.

have you seen the man under the bridge?
God had permitted him a good 43 years of live
Today would've been 51
But learning statistics of people like and around him,
Live beyond 50 was only a luxury

have you seen the man under the bridge?
I have not seen him in a while
His wife had long returned to the countryside
His youngest son has pursued his own fate
His neighbors still remember him
But no longer talk about him

These days I don't see him anymore
Now,
Have you seen the men under the bridge?
