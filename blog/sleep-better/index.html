Sleep Better

Fariz A.
2017-10-09

No coffee, cigarette or tea before bed

Prior to sleep, psychoactive and intoxicating substances should be avoided at any cost. Exceptions are those which is incorporated in the Sleep Better program such as herbs and supplements named below.

Exercise and Physical Actvity

Exercise the same day before sleep is important. It is best done in the morning. Just to make sure to have your last physically vigorous activity atleast 4-5 hours before sleep.

Have your last meal atleast 8 hours before waking up

This is important. Do not make stomach vigorously process food during sleep. Also make sure your body is ready to take a dump in the morning, a heavenly feeling.

But don’t sleep hungry either

Withstanding hunger can’t be done while trying sleep at to the same time. Make sure stomach has been filled prior to sleep.

Cut out refined carbs and sugar

Excessive glucose in blood creates lethargic feeling and may mess up with daily activities, including sleeping.

Cleanliness is next to godliness

Sleeping is a sacred activity and needs to be respected.

Bedroom enviroment setting

Dark room and warm bed. Also make sure room is clean, and has a good air circulation.

No blue screen min 2 hours prior to sleep

Blue screen meaning screen with blue light wavelength which is found in day sunlight. This messes up the natural circadian rhythm. Consider bluelight application for your device if you plan to use it at night.

Be wise about nap

Do not nap during the day. If you plan or have to do so, nap 15-30 minutes only, and only once a for that day.

Never go to the bed for anything else than for sleep

Do not mess with your brain by saying that bed doesn’t always equals sleep. Bed is used only and only for sleep.

Stress and anxiety must go

Finish all brain wrecker as soon as possible so you don’t have to deal with tangled stuffs at night. Consider meditation. Aromatherapy and many points said above are also meant to relaxe you and give the ultimate comfort.

Stick to a consistent schedule

This is not a one time instant trick. A good quality sleep is a series of habit.

Write on a sleep diary

Keep track of progress, evaluate your previous sleep and plan a better sleep for the next session.

Meditate

Sleeping is not only a physical thing, it’s also a mind game. Meditation teaches to totally be in control of your mind beside to keep you relaxed.

Mosquitos and Bugs free

Those basterds are one of the ultimate sleep buster. Consider safe bug repellent that doesn’t disturb your sleep attempt.

Dopamine is not so healthy-sleep-compatible

A feeling of euphoria creates a rush that prevents one from feeling asleep. Not only avoid to feel too depressed, also avoid to feel too happy.

Ask close relatives around you for support and to accompany

A better sleep is a not so easy process for many people. From a technical point of view, alarm sometimes isn’t smart enough to perform the wake up thingy.
